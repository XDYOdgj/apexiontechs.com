Secopad Baby Bath Mat with Suction Cups and Drain Holes

Make bath time a magical and safe experience for your little one with the Secopad baby bath mat. Featuring a delightful cartoon of pink sea animals, this mat adds a splash of fun and color to your child's bathroom. The non-slip surface ensures your toddler stays secure while playing or learning to bathe, providing peace of mind for parents.

The mat is designed with suction cups on the bottom, giving it a strong grip on smooth surfaces so it won't slide around.
